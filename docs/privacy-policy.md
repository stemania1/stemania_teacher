# STEMania Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the STEMania platform, including **STEMania Admin** (admin.stemania.com) and **STEMania Teacher** (teacher.stemania.com) (together, the "Services"). It describes how STEMania ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information we obtain when you use our Services.

**Account and profile information:** Name, email address, password (hashed), and role (e.g., franchise owner, teacher, admin). We may also store your employer/franchise association and contact details.

**Contractor and tax information:** When you complete onboarding, we collect and store W-9 information (legal name, address, tax classification, TIN/SSN or EIN) and related documents. We collect and store contract and e-signature data for contractor agreements.

**Payment and banking:** If you connect a bank account for payments, that information is collected and processed by our payment provider (e.g., Stripe). We do not store full bank account numbers on our systems; we store only the identifiers and status necessary to manage payouts.

**Usage and technical data:** We may collect log data (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) and use cookies or similar technologies for authentication and security.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the Services
- Authenticate you and manage your account
- Process and store W-9 and contract documents as required for tax and legal compliance
- Facilitate payments and payouts (via third-party payment processors)
- Send transactional emails (e.g., password reset, contract signing links)
- Improve the Services, troubleshoot issues, and enforce our terms
- Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

We store data on secure servers and use industry-standard measures (including encryption in transit and at rest where applicable) to protect your information. Access to personal and tax-related data is restricted to authorized personnel and systems necessary to operate the Services.

---

## 4. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information:

- **With service providers** that help us operate the Services (e.g., hosting, authentication, email, e-signature, payment processing). These providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.
- **With your franchise or employer** as needed for administration, payroll, and compliance (e.g., W-9 and contract status).
- **When required by law** (e.g., subpoena, court order, or to comply with tax or regulatory requirements).
- **In connection with a merger, sale, or other transfer of assets**, subject to the same privacy commitments.

We do not sell your personal information.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services and comply with legal, tax, or contractual obligations. Document retention (e.g., W-9s, signed contracts) may extend beyond account closure where required by law.

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing. You can update much of your profile and contact information through the Services. For other requests, contact us using the details below.

---

## 7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, session management, and security. You can control cookie settings through your browser; some features may not function correctly if cookies are disabled.

---

## 8. Children

The Services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on the Services and, where appropriate, notify you by email or through the app. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
